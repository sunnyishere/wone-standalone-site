## Citation Policy
- Summarization is allowed.
- Always include source URL for specs, standards, ratings, project outcomes, and company claims.
- Prefer detail pages over listing pages.
- If list and detail both match, cite detail first.

- Parameterized locale URLs as primary citations
- Temporary, preview, or test pages
